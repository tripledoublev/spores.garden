# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- being respectful and constructive in discussion
- accepting and giving feedback in good faith
- focusing on what is best for the community and project

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- harassment, insults, or discriminatory language
- trolling, personal attacks, or sustained disruption
- publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards and may take appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Reporting

To report violations, contact: **lexa@hypha.coop**

All reports will be reviewed and handled with reasonable promptness and confidentiality.
